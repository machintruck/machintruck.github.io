IoBee

Posted by Arthur von Kaenel on Nov 20, 2022

IoBee is an IOT image-processing bee counter. This device helps beekeepers keep track of the hive's activity, recording the number of bee entrances an exits every hour. On top of reducing the need for frequent long-distance trips to monitor individual hives, it alerts the keeper as soon as swarming behavior starts, leading to a higher likelihood of swarm re-capture.
